# Audio Setup Instructions

To add background music and sound effects to this project, follow these steps:

1. Place your audio files inside this `audio/` folder.
2. Rename your files so they match the expected names in the code, or update `script.js` to match your filenames.

### Recommended Files:
- **bgm.mp3** - Soft, cute background music (plays on loop after the first interaction).
- **click.mp3** - A small pop or click sound when the user advances the dialogue.
- **confetti.mp3** - A celebratory sound for the final scene (Scene 8).

### Modifying Code:
Open `script.js` and look at the top where the `Howl` objects are initialized. You can change the filenames there if you'd prefer to use different names.
